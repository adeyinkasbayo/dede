NEW FORMULA:
===========
Cash Balance = Opening Balance + Transfer to Staff - Total Winnings - Total Expenses - Daily Debt - Closing Balance

EXAMPLE:
- Opening: $1000
- Transfer to Staff: $200
- Winnings: $100
- Expenses: $50
- Daily Debt: $30
- Closing: $900
- Cash Balance = 1000 + 200 - 100 - 50 - 30 - 900 = $120
